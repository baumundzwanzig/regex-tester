Test file for regex: @listitem\((.*)\)

Sample content with @listitem patterns:

@listitem(first item)
Some text here
@listitem(second item with spaces)
More text
@listitem(third_item_with_underscores)
And even more text
@listitem(fourth item (with nested parentheses))

Also some false positives:
@listitem without parentheses
listitem(without @)
@listitem( incomplete

And some edge cases:
@listitem()
@listitem(a)
@listitem(very long item description with multiple words and punctuation!)
